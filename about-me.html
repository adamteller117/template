This is Adam Teller

Collaborator · Creative Thinker · Designer




Collaborator

I am naturally friendly with a personable and positive attitude that has allowed me to interact and successfully collaborate with Managers, C-Suite Executives, and Software Engineers.


As a collaborator, I always make sure that my users are being heard and that their opinion matters. That being said I am a firm believer in transparency and with that builds trust and communication. If there is a design or certain implementation that is not to someone's liking, I am very open to criticism. In my belief, I can only grow if I can learn from mistakes. From graduating from an engineering school I have learned to analyze mistakes by asking, "How did this happen?", "Where did I go wrong?", and "What is that I can do to make sure this does not happen again?".

Creative Thinker

I am naturally empathetic which has given me the ability to take myself out of my shoes and into the user's perspective. My Engineering and Psychology background enables me to speak the language of a Developer and a User Experience Researcher, while at the same time considering the needs of the stakeholder and users.


A creative thinker must be meticulous with their work. With that being said, I am very detail-oriented and I like to think deeply about design problems including every little decision detail.

Designer

As a designer I am open-minded and I continuously learn new frameworks and languages in order to better create the stakeholder's vision. I am never afraid of a new challenge but instead openly welcome it. From working at a startup for 4 years (2 internships and nearly 2 years full-time), ambiguous and daunting tasks are not unheard of in my line of work.


Recently, I have been reorganizing my thoughts and publishing them to my blog on Medium. Please check out my latest article, My thoughts and experiences from working for a startup.
